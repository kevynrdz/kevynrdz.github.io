About the studio

Firebirdz Games is an indiependient solo game studio founded by KevynRdz in Misiones, Argentina. It was born with the idea of design, develop, publish and promote its own and third-party video games on different internet platforms, as well as offering conceptual art, 2d/3d design, music production, and many others services.
